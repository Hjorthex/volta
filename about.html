About Volta

"The language of experiment is more authoritative than any reasoning..."

- Alessandro Volta Inventor of the battery

Volta is a new innovative brand, founded in 2020, our vision is to create a new future for the car industry. Our brand values an exclusive design, comfort and sustainability. Volta V1 is the start of a new electrical age for Volta. The soft shapes make the car look both sporty and elegant, and this is further enhanced by the modern body colors. Precise lines emphasize the amazing dynamic potential of the fully electric motor. The cockpit of the Volta V1 is toned down and consistently oriented towards the driver with the large Curved Display as the central display unit. At the same time, the bright and open cabin with continuous panoramic glass roof fits perfectly with the calm and superb electric motor.

On the way through the city in the morning, off to the coast with the whole family on the weekends, or quickly from A to B after dark - your Volta V1 does it all. It accompanies you through all facets of life. Not just as a car, but as a loyal and faithful companion. With tailor-made accessories and original parts, we make sure that you are perfectly equipped for both small and large challenges. At the same time, we offer a range of services so you can concentrate on what is most important - pure driving pleasure.

Although electric cars still have their big breakthrough for good, they are slowly gaining more and more traction on the roads. But the challenge of getting the cars to run far enough on a single charge, and being able to charge the battery fast enough, still holds many back from throwing themselves into an electric car adventure. To solve that problem, we, Volta, will now explore the possibility of developing roads that charge the cars while they drive, using induction technology.
